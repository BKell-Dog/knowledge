The most common type of electrical connections are pins and sockets, and these are used in breadboarding and prototyping. A pin is a metal shaft, either circular or square, and a socket is a recetacle for a pin.

This is a basic pin header:
![Pin Header Example](https://www.smcomponents.com.au/cdn/shop/products/57_c5af96f5-e302-4d0d-9ed2-318ec985a9c1.jpg?v=1677276067&width=1445)

 For hobbyists the short ends of these pins are typically soldered onto a PCB, so that it can be inserted into a breadboard.

![Arduino Nano](https://image.lceda.cn/pullimage/XfU8ZpsSOWO47naKWT22mSOAvJm8VHGb4pqSPmEH.jpeg)
